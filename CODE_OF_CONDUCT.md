# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free project space.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical issues.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive behavior that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors who violate this policy.
